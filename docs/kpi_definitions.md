# KPI Definitions

Canonical definitions for all metrics used across dashboards and SQL queries.
These definitions are the single source of truth — any deviation in a dashboard
should be flagged as a data quality issue, not treated as an alternate view.

---

## Revenue Metrics

### Net Revenue

> The actual amount received from a sale after applying discounts.

```
Net Revenue = unit_price × quantity × (1 − discount)
```

- **Excludes:** taxes, returns, chargebacks, shipping fees
- **Grain:** Transaction line item
- **Used in:** All dashboards, all SQL files
- **Note:** Use `net_revenue` column in `sales_transactions` (pre-computed during cleaning)

---

### Gross Profit

> Net revenue minus the cost of goods sold.

```
Gross Profit = Net Revenue − (unit_cost × quantity)
```

- **unit_cost** sourced from `products` table (joined at query time)
- **Excludes:** Operating expenses, SG&A, overhead — this is not operating profit

---

### Gross Margin %

> Gross profit expressed as a percentage of net revenue.

```
Gross Margin % = (Gross Profit / Net Revenue) × 100
```

- **Target:** > 20% overall; > 30% for Technology category
- **Alert threshold:** < 10% signals aggressive discounting or cost overrun

---

### Average Order Value (AOV)

> Net revenue per transaction.

```
AOV = SUM(net_revenue) / COUNT(DISTINCT transaction_id)
```

- **Note:** Calculated at the customer, segment, region, or overall level
- **Not** the average product price — it is the average total per order

---

### Year-over-Year (YoY) Growth

> Revenue change vs. the same period in the prior year.

```
YoY Growth % = (Current Period Revenue − Prior Year Revenue) / Prior Year Revenue × 100
```

- **Period:** Typically month, quarter, or full year
- **Positive** = growth; **Negative** = decline

---

## Customer Metrics

### Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)

> Total net revenue generated by a single customer across all transactions to date.

```
LTV = SUM(net_revenue) WHERE customer_id = X
```

- **Not** a predicted future value — this is historical LTV only
- To compute predicted LTV: multiply AOV × purchase frequency × expected lifespan

---

### Revenue per Customer

> Average revenue across all customers in a group.

```
Revenue per Customer = SUM(net_revenue) / COUNT(DISTINCT customer_id)
```

---

### RFM Scores

| Dimension | Definition | Scoring |
|---|---|---|
| **Recency (R)** | Days since last order (reference = MAX order_date in dataset) | 1–5; 5 = most recent |
| **Frequency (F)** | COUNT(DISTINCT transaction_id) per customer | 1–5; 5 = most frequent |
| **Monetary (M)** | SUM(net_revenue) per customer | 1–5; 5 = highest spend |

RFM Total = R + F + M (range: 3–15)

Scores are computed using `NTILE(5)` window functions — see `08_customer_segmentation.sql`.

---

## Operational Metrics

### Average Days to Ship

> Mean number of calendar days between order placement and shipment.

```
Avg Days to Ship = AVG(DATE_DIFF('day', order_date, ship_date))
```

- **Target:** ≤ 3 days
- **Alert:** > 7 days in any region signals fulfilment issues

---

### Discount Rate

> Average discount fraction applied across transactions.

```
Avg Discount Rate = AVG(discount) × 100   -- expressed as %
```

- **Healthy range:** 5–15%
- **Risk zone:** > 20% consistently signals margin erosion
